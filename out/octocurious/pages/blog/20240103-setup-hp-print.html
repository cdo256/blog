AI writing tools are software applications that leverage artificial intelligence, machine learning, and natural language processing to generate human-like text. They can assist in various writing tasks, from generating social media posts to creating SEO-optimized content. Some of these tools also have the capability to reference other articles or sources, which can be particularly useful for creating citation-rich content[1][2][4][12].

Here are some AI writing tools that can reference other articles:

  1. AI Writer: This tool starts the writing process with a Google search, pulling in source materials to write an article. It provides citations, allowing users to trace back everything the AI writes to the original sources[2].
  2. Frase: Aimed at content marketers and SEO professionals, Frase is structured around a framework of content brief, content writing, content optimization, and content analytics. It excels in research and brief outlines[4].
  3. ContentShake: This all-in-one AI article writer is designed to automate the entire content production process. It provides recommendations for SEO, helps optimize drafts, and publishes articles to your blog[6].
  4. Writesonic: This tool offers a simple process for content creation and supports multiple languages. It has a long-form assistant for creating longer pieces of content[10].
  5. Rytr: An AI writing assistant that helps create high-quality content. It can write SEO-optimized meta titles for your website and blog posts[9].
  6. Jasper AI: Known for its amazing features and templates, it's easy to use and can generate a variety of content[10].

Remember, while these tools can assist in the writing process, they are not perfect and the output often needs to be edited for coherence, style, and SEO optimization. They should be seen as writing partners rather than replacements for human writers[6][10]. As an open-source developer with an interest in AI, you might find these tools useful in generating content, brainstorming ideas, or even in developing your own AI-powered operating system.
